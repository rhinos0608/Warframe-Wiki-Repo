# Peram Fishing Spear

This fishing spear has multiple blades that allow it to pierce the hides of scaly fish. 

Obtained from Fisher Hai-Luk in Cetus (Earth). This spear is most effective on the following Plains of Eidolon fish:
Glappid
Khut-Khut
Mawfish
Mortus Lungfish
Norg
