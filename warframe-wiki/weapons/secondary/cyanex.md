# Cyanex

Sentient tech merges with Corpus design to deliver this lethal sidearm. Fully automatic, it fires ricocheting, homing projectiles that release small clouds of <DT_GAS_COLOR>Gas on impact. Alt-fire to release the entire magazine without homing as a single burst.
