# Mire

A relic from the Great Plague, the Mire Infested Sword is a brutal weapon. It not only cuts into the enemy, it infects the wounds of its victim with a toxic nerve poison. Deals <DT_POISON_COLOR>Toxin Damage on Ground Slam attacks.
