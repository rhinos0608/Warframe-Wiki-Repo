# Gazal Machete

Each kill with this swooping blade adds bonus damage to the Djinn Sentinel’s next Fatal Attraction ability. In turn, Fatal Attraction bestows the weapon with a period of <DT_CORROSIVE_COLOR>Corrosive Damage.
