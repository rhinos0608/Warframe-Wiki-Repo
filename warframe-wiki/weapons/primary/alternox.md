# Alternox

Gyre’s signature weapon has two fire modes. Primary fire shocks enemies with electrical orbs. Alternate fire is a large ball of electricity that sticks to any surface and pulses <DT_ELECTRICITY_COLOR>Electricity before it explodes. In Gyre's hands, the weapon has a small amount of multishot.
