# Nagantaka

Slash targets with a single precise bolt from Garuda's signature crossbow. Alt-fire to let loose a barrage of bolts. All bolts have a chance to cause <DT_SLASH_COLOR>Slash Status, and Headshots have a chance to increase Reload Speed. When wielded by Garuda Nagantaka gains a slight Punch Through.
