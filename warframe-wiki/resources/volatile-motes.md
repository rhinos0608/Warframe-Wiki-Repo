# Volatile Motes

Highly charged particles of pure energy. The source of the Jade Light, and Jade's power. Highly coveted by Parvos Granum for his Jade Eximus program.

Obtained from Operation Ascension missions (Brutus on Uranus) and special Operation Alerts during Operation: Belly of the Beast.
