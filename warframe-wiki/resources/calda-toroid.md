# Calda Toroid

A device of undetermined function and origin. Prized by Vox Solaris and the Quills.

Earn Calda Toroids from enemies located at the Enrichment Labs in the Orb Vallis and from Scyto Raknoids. They can also be found as pick-ups in caves located around the Orb Vallis (Venus).
