# Sola Toroid

A device of undetermined function and origin. Prized by Vox Solaris and the Quills.

Earn Sola Toroids from enemies located at the Temple of Profit in the Orb Vallis and from Kyta Raknoids. They can also be found as pick-ups in caves located around the Orb Vallis (Venus).
