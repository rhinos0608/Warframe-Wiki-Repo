# Murkray Liver

An ingredient used in Ostron cooking and medicine.

Source: Cut from Murkray fish via Fisher Hai-Luk in Cetus (Earth). Catch Murkray in Plains of Eidolon ocean hotspots at any time of day using Murkray Bait. Also drops from Thumpers in the Plains of Eidolon.
